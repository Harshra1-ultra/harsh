About Me

I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. in the Department of Electronics and Electrical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati, since July 2022, under the guidance of Dr. Manoj B. R. I earned my M.Tech. in Communication and Signal Processing from the Indian Institute of Technology Ropar in 2022, and my B.E. in Electronics and Communication Engineering from the University Institute of Technology Burdwan in 2019.
